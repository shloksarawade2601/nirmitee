We at NIRMITEE create ideas and strive to transform them into reality

At NIRMITEE, we believe in transforming ideas into reality. Since its inception, Nirmitee has been a vibrant platform for civil engineering enthusiasts to showcase their brilliance, knowledge, and skills at their highest potential.

We bring you an exciting lineup of events, including Box Cricket, Technoquiz, Paper Presentation, Treasure Hunt, CV Cruitz, Bob the Builder, and E-Tendering. These competitions, spanning both technical and fun domains, are designed to test participants’ competence, creativity, teamwork, and logical thinking abilities.
